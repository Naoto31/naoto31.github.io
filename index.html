Greetings from Munich!

- Life is an adventure -

I love to code, design and help to tell your ideas attractively.

Originally my tech path started with a masters in mechanical engineering at Niigata University in Japan. As I am also interested in PR & Storytelling, I spent my first years after university as a Consultant in this field. Nevertheless, my core interest was always the technological aspect of a company’s story and how it can be told. In order to learn more about this, I spent my spare time on online courses and applied my new knowledge in own projects. After studying python for a while and receiving a “Professional Data Scientist Certification”, I happened to come across some Web Development tasks. I quickly realized that Web Development joins all aspects I am passionate about. It is something in which I can write code, be creative and tell somebodies story in a way that it catches others attention.

Besides my profession, I write articles in a magazine for Japanese living in Germany and enjoy sharing my trips and life in Germany with my wife on our YouTube channel and website.
